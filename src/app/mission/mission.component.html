Diversity
We are an Equal Oportunity Employer and hire regardless of race, gender, sexual orientation and more. We make sure that we create a inclusive environment for both our employees and clients alike. Carolina Construction has taken the pledge with the Associated General Contractors of America to create an inclusive, welcoming environment to advance diverse talent in the construction industry.
Environmental Intiative
Construction is a major contributor to greenhouse gases, making up around 37% of global emissions. The production and use of materials including cement, aluminum, and even steel have a substantial carbon footprint. Carolina Construction is focused on reducing emission by adhering to policies such as carbon pricing and using renewable fuel for equipment.
